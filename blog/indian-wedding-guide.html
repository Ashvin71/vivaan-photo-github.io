Planning Guide

Indian Wedding Photography Guide

Indian weddings move quickly and beautifully. The best photos come from planning the timeline, family portraits, rituals, lighting, and travel before the celebration begins.

Pre-Wedding Events

Mehndi, sangeet, haldi, garba, and rehearsal events each have their own rhythm. Build a photo timeline that leaves space for decor, entrances, family, dance, and candid moments.

Wedding Day

For the baraat, ceremony, portraits, and reception, choose photo locations early and keep family photo lists ready. This helps everyone stay calm and present.

Tips for Couples

Share your event schedule, important traditions, VIP family names, and must-have portraits before the wedding week. Good planning lets the day feel natural.
