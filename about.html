Our Story

About Vivaan Photo

Vivaan Photo is a professional wedding photography and cinematography company serving couples in the USA since 2008. Our work is rooted in calm direction, strong timing, clean portraits, and a deep respect for family, emotion, and tradition.

Based in Springfield, Illinois, Vivaan Photo serves Indian, American, multicultural, and fusion weddings across Springfield, St. Louis, the Chicago suburbs, the Midwest, USA, and Canada.

Photography Services

Your satisfaction is our first priority. Vivaan Photo offers a complete package of contemporary, traditional, photojournalistic, artistic, and modern wedding photography styles.

We are fully equipped with high-end cameras, backup equipment, professional lighting, lenses, audio tools, and trained technicians. Our goal is to deliver professional quality photos with a unique touch, where each image tells the story of a song, laughter, or a tear.

Our professional photographers and videographers are highly experienced with weddings, engagements, family portraits, events, and post-production services. We capture your day with a photojournalistic approach and minimal interruption so you can enjoy every moment with family and friends.

Whether you need photography, cinematography, albums, or a complete wedding story from start to finish, Vivaan Photo creates customized packages designed around your events, location, timeline, and final delivery needs.
